# Aliolo Subscription Terms

These terms describe paid premium access for Aliolo.

## Scope

Subscription access applies to premium features, services, or other paid
functionality that Aliolo may provide from time to time.

## Billing

Subscriptions may be billed through the relevant app store or billing provider.
Price, billing cadence, renewal behavior, taxes, and local currency handling are
controlled by the purchase channel and the active offer shown to the customer.

## Cancellation

Users can cancel according to the rules of the purchase channel. Cancellation
stops future renewal, but access already paid for continues until the end of the
current billing period unless the purchase channel states otherwise.

## Refunds

Refunds, chargebacks, and local consumer rights are handled according to the
purchase channel and applicable law.

## Access Changes

If a subscription ends or is not renewed, Aliolo may suspend or remove access
to premium features and services while leaving any free features available.

## Changes

Aliolo may update these terms to reflect product, legal, or billing changes.
The current version should always be kept in sync with the product checkout and
store listing copy.

This document is a product policy draft and should be reviewed by legal counsel
before publication.
